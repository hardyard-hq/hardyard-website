The Shield & Tool Protection
The Shield is HardYard's tool protection system. You register each tool with its name, serial number, estimated value, a photo and a receipt if you have one. That data is encrypted and stored securely against your account.

If your tools get lifted, you can generate a full Police Pack PDF in one tap — a professional document with serial numbers, photos, values and your details, ready to hand to the police or your insurance company.

You can also mark a tool as stolen which triggers a live alert to other HardYard users in your area. If someone tries to register that serial number on another account, it flags immediately.
Open The Shield, find the tool, tap Mark as Stolen. That triggers three things:

1. A live stolen alert goes out to other HardYard users nearby.
2. The serial number is flagged — if anyone tries to register it on another account, HardYard catches it.
3. You can generate a Police Pack PDF instantly — all your tool details, serial numbers, photos and proof of purchase in one professional document.

The more tradespeople who register their tools on The Shield, the harder it becomes to sell stolen kit. That's the network effect.
The App
The CSCS Card Wallet lets you store a digital copy of your CSCS card (or White Card in Australia, OSHA card in the US) directly in the app.

Add your card number, card type, expiry date and photographs of the front and back. When a site manager asks to see your card, tap Show Card for a full-screen display — bright screen, easy to read, no fumbling in your wallet in the rain.

The app will warn you when your card is approaching its expiry date so you never get turned away at the gate.
